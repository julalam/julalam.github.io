My Kids, My Passion!

I enjoy many things in life, but what I really enjoy the most is spending time with my family. I have two wonderful girls and they are the most important part of my life. While observing my daughters learning life, I felt strong need to preserve the most precious moments. Because of that photography appeared in my life. Through lens of my camera I can tell a new story every day. With help of Photoshop I love to make my pictures look even more magical. And my digital drawing tablet allows me to creatively manipulate photos and explore the artist in myself. For me photography is a opportunity to express my creativity needs. One of my dreams is to make a custom illustrated children book which would combine my children pictures and illustrations in a beautiful and instructive story.
