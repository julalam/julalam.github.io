My Life in Stitches

Cross stitching is one of my favorite hobbies along with photography. I have been cross stitching since I was nine and the amount of completed pieces is hard to count. Cross stitching is a very concentrative heavy task but it is also very relaxing. Counted cross stitches require a huge amount of organizational skills. Doing large projects that takes from several months to years, I learned how to get organized and be patient. Despite of all complexity, nicety and long duration of working process, it is a good rest for me. It’s always exciting to make first stitch and see how bit by bit a new beautiful embroidery appears. Completed pieces I always frame and decorate my walls with them or give to my friends or donate to charity auctions.
